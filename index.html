Notes & Assumptions More info
  • Assumptions: one full cycle per day; no degradation; no manufacturing/transport emissions.
  • Emission factors used (for comparison): Brown coal EF = 336.6 kg CO₂/MWh; Black coal EF = 324 kg CO₂/MWh.
  • Offset formula used: CE = (C × 365 × EF × Nyears × Nbatteries) / 1000
  • ROI formulas: Payback = Cost / Annual Revenue; ROI Improvement (%) = (1 − PaybackOptimised/PaybackNormal) × 100
